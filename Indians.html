History

Baseball in Cleveland started in the late 1800s but the name Cleveland Indians came in 1916 when the sold their best player(he was the name of the team) and asked clevelander's what they thought the new name should be the pick the cleveland indians. They have been the 6 world series and won 2 of them. They have a rich history and are one of baseballs oldest teams.
